José Roberto Pinto da Silva, Ph.D.

Who Am I?

I was born in Santiago, Rio Grande do Sul - Brazil. I am an Electrical Engineer, Ph.D. and MBA - with an emphasis in Electric Power Systems and Control Systems.

I started my studies in Electrical Engineering at Universidade Federal do Paraná. In the meantime, I did internships at different companies and had various important roles. From programmer to project manager - and more - I always did my job the best way possible, communicating well with other employees and finding the best solution to the problem. Afterward, I decided to continue my studies, getting my master's, doctorate, MBA, and other leadership courses.

Still, as a student, I became an assistant professor and that way I started my career as a professor. I taught Electrical Engineering subjects for 41 years at UFPR, where I received many awards. I also taught at Universidade Positivo as a Post-Graduate professor and at some international seminaries.

My career as an engineer started as a student still - at Siderúrgica Guaíra. After working at other big named companies, I decided to continue my career at COPEL Transmissão e Geração S.A. At COPEL, I was a Senior Engineer and I worked there for 41 years.

I am also a co-author of technical articles, being part of seminaries, congresses, and courses for CIGRÉ Brasil, and a member of the Committee of Project Analysis for Research and Development.
